ABOUT RAMA BEN KHALIFA

My name is Rama Ben Khalifa. Until a few days ago I would have called myself a party girl, present tense. I'm not sure that's honest anymore, and I'm not sure it's fully untrue either — which is the whole point of this site.

I'm a Gemini sun, which means I contain multitudes and lose track of most of them. I'm a Scorpio moon and Scorpio rising, which means whatever I feel, I feel at full volume, and whatever I hide, I hide well. Type B on paper. Borderline underneath. Chaotic by instinct, and, I'd like to think, still capable of class.

I've been in and out of this lifestyle for years — on for a while, off for a while, on again. It has cost me things. Friendships. Trust. At points, my own legal standing. This isn't a redemption tour and I'm not asking to be forgiven on a schedule. I just got tired of other people telling my story worse than I could tell it myself.

So: a magazine, not a diary. Stories you can click into, one at a time, the way you'd flip to a page that has your name on it. Some are funny. Some are not flattering. All of them are mine.

This is Rama Ben Khalifa, reforming a party girl, in real time, in public, on purpose.
